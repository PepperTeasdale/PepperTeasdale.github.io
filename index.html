About Me

I'm a Brooklyn-based web developer with experience in Ruby, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Python, and frameworks such as Rails and React.js. Ever since my dad brought home our first PC when I was in grade school, I have been interested in computers and wrote my first programs in BASIC. I got into music in High School and college, but my passion for computers persisted in using computers for recording and arranging. I worked in music for some years after college, before moving to NYC.

More recently I decided to make a career shift and get into web development. I taught myself Python for a year before enrolling in App Academy in fall 2015. My current stack includes Ruby (on Rails), JavaScript (React.js & jQuery), HTML/CSS, and SQL (Postgres), but I am eager and willing to learn whatever technologies the job calls for. Initially I was more interested in backend work, but I have developed a love for creating dynamic user interfaces with JavaScript as well.
